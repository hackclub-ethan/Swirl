What Swirl Is

Swirl is a YSWS by Hackclub. Every 3 unique features a website has the larger the grant is. The grant is meant for the Hackcluber to get Icecream.

A unique feature is something that is not a base requirement. Some of the base requirements are:

  • Favicon
  • Image
  • Title
  • And more

The more scoops you have the larger the grant. The base grant is $5 USD and each additional scoop (up to 3 total) adds $2 USD each!
